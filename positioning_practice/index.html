Overview

So You Think You Can Dance is an American dance competition show. The twelfth season returned on Monday, June 1, 2015. The show was aired every Monday at 8:00PM eastern time.

Auditions were held in Dallas, Texas; Detroit, Michigan; Los Angeles, California; Memphis, Tennessee; and New York, New York.

This season on SYTYCD there was a complete shift in the competition format. Dancers auditioned to be on the stage team or the street team. The stage team included styles such as: ballet, contemporary, tao, jazz, and latin. The street team included styles such as: freestyle, hip-hop, and krump. The top 20 finalist were made up of 10 stage dancers and 10 street dancers.

Each week the bottom three stage and street dancers were selected. The public had the chance to save their favorite street and stage dancer in the bottom by live tweeting who they wanted to save. The dancer witht the most tweets was saved and the judges would save one more street and stage dance. Once the competition reacher top 10 they were eleminated only on the votes from the public.

Voting took place by going onto fox.com, using the Fox Now App to super vote, or by SMS.

This years winner was Gaby Diaz from Miami, FL. She auditioned twice this season as a tap dancer and got a ticket to Vegas after her second audition. Her versatality won over the judges and America, landing her a spot as a dancer in J. Lo's Vegas show.

Judges

Jason Derulo is an American singer, songwriter and dancer. He has now become a permanent judge on So You Think You Can Dance.As of 2009 he has become a solo recording artist and has sold over 50 million singles as well as achieved ten career platinum singles, including "Wiggle", "Talk Dirty", "In My Head", and "Whatcha Say".

Nigel Lyrhgoe is an English television and film director and producer, television dance competition judge, former dancer in the Young Generation and choreographer. He is noted for being the producer of the shows Pop Idol and American Idol and creator and executive producer of, and a regular judge on So You Think You Can Dance.

Paula Abdul is an American singer-songwriter, dancer, choreographer, actress and television personality. Before rising to stardom she was a highly sought-after choreographer at the height of the music video era. Abdul later scored a string of pop music hits in the late 1980s and early 1990s. She has now become a permanent judge on So You Think You Can Dance.

Host

Cat Deeley is an English television presenter, actress, singer, and model. She has returned for her 11th consecutive season of So You Think You Can Dance and will be expecting a baby in the coming year.

Captains

Travis Wall was the team captain for Team Stage. He is a dancer and dance instructor, with a specialty in contemporary and jazz. In 2006, he competeted on So You Think You Can Dance Season 2 and was the runner up. As of 2012, he is a choreographer for the show. In 2011, he was nominated for an Emmy for his work on Season 7.

Stephen "Twitch" Boss was the captain for Team Steet. He is a freestyle hip-hop dancer and was runner up for So you Think You Can Dance Season 4 in 2008. He has appeared in several dance realted movies including: Step Up 3D, Step Up Revolution, Step Up: All In, and Magic Mike XXL.
